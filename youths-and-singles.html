YOUTH & SINGLE

The Lord holds a special place in his heart for young and single people. Losing your youth means losing your future. We will work to reach out to college campuses and prepare young people for the spiritual challenges they will face in the world. We will not let darkness overpower our light. Let us focus on mentoring in Spirit and truth.

We must not overlook the population segment that includes youths, singles, and young adults due to the world's numerous negative influences and pressures today. As we sleep, groups are investing heavily in trapping our future. However, we cannot just sit idly and watch our future be destroyed. We must work to save our youth from the overwhelming influences and pressures that seek to ensnare them.
