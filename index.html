ENOCH & FRIENDS FOUNDATION (E&FF)

The Enoch & Friends Foundation, a non-profit organization based on the Christian faith, was registered in Maryland, USA, in 2019. Its mission is to reach millions of people worldwide through outreach efforts. Enoch Akinsola has a special mission to the world based on Acts 10:38. His goal is to encourage a global audience to follow in the footsteps of the Lord and carry out His teachings as commanded in John 14:12.

True and authentic friendships are more important than ever in today's world. We are faced with many challenges that can feel overwhelming, but with the support of real friends, we can create a better world for ourselves and others. Jesus Christ referred to His disciples as friends, and we, too, can strive to be friends by doing good and living as the works of God, as stated in Ephesians 2:10.

Who are the friends in this idea? They are spiritual partners, collaborators, and givers or donors who will support each other through prayer and assistance whenever needed. Anyone who receives assistance will also be considered a partner on this journey. Please make sure to attend all meetings that will be scheduled by COVENANT PARTNERS, ee which is the ministry branch of the foundation.

These are the five ways we shall be operating

HEAR MY CRY, O LORD!- It has been announced that God will grant the prayers of married couples waiting for children. This special event will happen once a year in various locations around the world, where God will intervene and help solve complicated issues. Just like in the Bible story of Hannah, many families will finally receive their heart's desires. Be prepared for this miraculous event.


GLOBAL MIRACLE DANCE- Welcome to the Thanksgiving ministry! Our annual praise, worship, and testimony night promises to be a grand event. It's a miraculous gathering where many people will dance into their long-awaited miracles. Get ready for unusual miracles, signs, and wonders!


FAMILY & MARRIAGE- WHEN IT CAN TAKE ONLY GOD!- If anything hinders your desire to settle down, trust that God will handle it. Spiritual complications can sometimes prevent people from getting married, but God has said that it's not good for man to be alone, and finding a wife is a good thing that brings favor from the Lord. We rely on these scriptures and the ministry of the Holy Spirit to help those who are sincere and ready to find a partner for marriage.

BETHESDA, THE HEALING POOL- God has never ceased to heal people, so the healing pool is always active. To receive healing, one must have faith and a strong desire for it. God has designated specific vessels for different individuals and circumstances. You may receive healing through one of these channels, and it will be a cherished experience.


YOUTHS & SINGLES- The Lord holds a special place in his heart for young people and single people. Losing your youth means losing your future. We will work to reach out to college campuses and prepare young people for the spiritual challenges they will face in the world. We will not let darkness overpower our light. Let us focus on mentoring in Spirit and truth.
