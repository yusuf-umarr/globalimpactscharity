ENOCH & FRIENDS FOUNDATION (E&FF)

The Enoch & Friends Foundation, a non-profit organization based on the Christian faith, was registered in Maryland, USA, in 2019. Its mission is to reach millions of people worldwide through outreach efforts. Enoch Akinsola has a special mission to the world based on Acts 10:38. His goal is to encourage a global audience to follow in the footsteps of the Lord and carry out His teachings as commanded in John 14:12.

True and authentic friendships are more important than ever in today's world. We are faced with many challenges that can feel overwhelming, but with the support of real friends, we can create a better world for ourselves and others. Jesus Christ referred to His disciples as friends, and we, too, can strive to be friends by doing good and living as the works of God, as stated in Ephesians 2:10.

Who are the friends in this idea? They are spiritual partners, collaborators, and givers or donors who will support each other through prayer and assistance whenever needed. Anyone who receives assistance will also be considered a partner on this journey. Please make sure to attend all meetings that will be scheduled by COVENANT PARTNERS, which is the ministry branch of the foundation.



COVENANT PARTNERS'S MANDATE

ACTS 10:38
The Ministry's assignments are based on Acts 10:38, as God never sends anyone without a word. As an escape route, I registered Enoch & Friends Foundation in Maryland, USA, in 2019 to do other things besides the Ministry's work. I have substituted other things to replace the work of the Ministry in the last twenty-four years. I have had fliers for all these programs designed since 2015, but I had done everything to escape this Heavenly call until I was compelled to do this because someone out there has cried to God. "How God anointed Jesus of Nazareth with the Holy Spirit and power, and how he went around doing good and healing all who were under the power of the devil, because God was with him." NIV "And you know that God anointed Jesus of Nazareth with the Holy Spirit and with power. Then Jesus went around doing good and healing all who were oppressed by the devil, for God was with him." NLT "How God anointed and consecrated Jesus of Nazareth with the [Holy] Spirit and with strength and ability and power; how He went about doing good and, [a]in particular, curing all who were harassed and oppressed by [the power of] the devil, for God was with Him." (ABCE) While Jesus was leaving the stage, He made a promise. John 14:12 (Amplified Bible, Classic Edition says, "I assure you, most solemnly I tell you, if anyone steadfastly believes in Me, he will himself be able to do the things that I do; and he will do even greater things than these, because I go to the Father." There are two major concerns in the scriptures above, i. You will do the works I do. ii. You will do greater work than this. How would you be able to do the works I do before thinking of doing greater work than this? You must be empowered with Acts 10:38 and align with Ephesians 2:10 10 For we are God's [own] handiwork (His workmanship), [d]recreated in Christ Jesus, [born anew] that we may do those good works which God predestined (planned beforehand) for us [taking paths which He prepared ahead of time], that we should walk in them [living the good life which He prearranged and made ready for us to live]. With these scriptural arsenals, the world is about to witness new but strange waves of revival. God will prove Himself as the faithful sender.
