BETHESDA, THE HEALING POOL

Throughout history, God has consistently healed people, which means the healing pool is constantly active. To receive healing, one must have faith and a strong desire for it. God has designated specific vessels for different individuals and circumstances. You may receive healing through one of these channels, which will be a cherished experience. This is one of the hallmarks of Jesus' assignment on the earth and is in line with the mandate given to Enoch to run following Acts 10:38.

Healing is beneficial, especially when medicine has exhausted all options or appears hopeless. Jesus healed everyone brought to Him. Healing still happens. The pool will be stirred yearly on this platform. If you have faith, you can achieve miracles in the kingdom. This is as commanded. The Holy Spirit guides individuals who are ready for their healing miracles. Those ready for their healing miracles will do whatever it takes to make it a date. It's important to remember that this event only takes place once a year.
